How to Take a Taxi in Korea

Complete guide for foreigners — Updated 2026

Recommended Taxi Apps

K-ride (by Kakao T)
The most popular taxi-hailing app in Korea. Works nationwide with English support. No Korean phone number required — sign up with email. You can set pickup/drop-off in English and pay by international credit card.
Google Play | App Store
TABA (Seoul Official Taxi App)
The official international tourist taxi app by Seoul Metropolitan Government. Designed specifically for foreign visitors. Supports English, Chinese, and Japanese. Operates in Seoul area.
Learn more on Seoul.go.kr

Hailing a Taxi

  • Look for the red 빈차 (vacant) sign on the taxi roof — it means the taxi is available.
  • Show your destination in Korean on your phone. Use Naver Map or KakaoMap — drivers rarely understand English addresses.
  • Doors are NOT automatic. Unlike Japan, you must open and close the door yourself.
  • At airports and major train stations, always use the official taxi stands. Never follow strangers offering rides.

Payment Methods

All taxis accept the following payment methods:

  • Cash (Korean Won)
  • Credit / Debit Card (Visa, Mastercard accepted)
  • T-money card (rechargeable transit card)
  • KakaoPay / Samsung Pay (if you have a Korean account)

No tipping is needed. Tipping is not customary in Korea.

Safety Tips

  1. Always check the meter is running. If the driver refuses to use the meter, get out and find another taxi.
  2. Use official taxi stands at airports and stations. Avoid unmarked vehicles.
  3. Decline flat-rate offers from drivers outside airports — metered fares are almost always cheaper.
  4. Official taxis have yellow license plates starting with 아, 바, 사, or 자.
  5. Report problems to the Tourism Hotline: 1330 (multilingual, available 24/7).

Panduan Taksi di Korea untuk Wisatawan Asing

Panduan lengkap berdasarkan pengalaman wisatawan — Diperbarui 2026

Aplikasi Taksi yang Direkomendasikan

K-ride (oleh Kakao T)
Aplikasi pemesanan taksi paling populer di Korea. Berfungsi di seluruh negeri dengan dukungan bahasa Inggris. Tidak perlu nomor telepon Korea — daftar dengan email. Bisa bayar dengan kartu kredit internasional.
Google Play | App Store
TABA (Aplikasi Taksi Resmi Seoul)
Aplikasi taksi resmi dari Pemerintah Kota Seoul, dirancang khusus untuk wisatawan asing. Mendukung bahasa Inggris, Mandarin, dan Jepang. Beroperasi di wilayah Seoul.
Info lebih lanjut di Seoul.go.kr

Cara Menghentikan Taksi

  • Cari tanda merah 빈차 (kosong) di atap taksi — artinya taksi tersedia.
  • Tunjukkan tujuan Anda dalam bahasa Korea di ponsel. Gunakan Naver Map atau KakaoMap — sopir jarang memahami alamat bahasa Inggris.
  • Pintu TIDAK otomatis. Berbeda dengan Jepang, Anda harus membuka dan menutup pintu sendiri.
  • Di bandara dan stasiun utama, selalu gunakan halte taksi resmi. Jangan ikut orang asing yang menawarkan tumpangan.

Tips Keamanan

  1. Selalu periksa argo berjalan. Jika sopir menolak menggunakan argo, turun dan cari taksi lain.

Ứng dụng gọi taxi được đề xuất

Ứng dụng gọi taxi phổ biến nhất Hàn Quốc. Hoạt động trên toàn quốc với hỗ trợ tiếng Anh. Không cần số điện thoại Hàn Quốc — đăng ký bằng email. Có thể thanh toán bằng thẻ tín dụng quốc tế.

Cách bắt taxi

  • Tìm biển đỏ 빈차 (trống) trên nóc taxi — nghĩa là taxi đang trống.
  • Cho tài xế xem điểm đến bằng tiếng Hàn trên điện thoại. Dùng Naver Map hoặc KakaoMap — tài xế hiếm khi hiểu địa chỉ tiếng Anh.
  • Cửa KHÔNG tự động. Khác với Nhật Bản, bạn phải tự mở và đóng cửa.
  • Tại sân bay và ga lớn, luôn sử dụng bến taxi chính thức. Đừng đi theo người lạ mời chào.
