Korea Transportation Guide

Everything you need to know about getting around Korea — Updated 2026

T-money Card

  • Buy at any convenience store (CU, GS25, 7-Eleven) for ₩4,000 (card fee)
  • Works on subway, bus, and taxi nationwide
  • ₩100 discount per ride compared to cash or single-use tickets
  • Enables free transfers between bus and subway within 30 minutes
  • Recharge with cash at convenience stores or subway ticket machines
  • Refundable at convenience stores or Incheon Airport (₩500 processing fee)
